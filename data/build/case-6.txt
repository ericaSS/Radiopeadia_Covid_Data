COVID-19 pneumonia - AGE : 30 years, GENDER : Male
Presentation : Young patient presents with high grade fever, chills and short of breath.
X-Ray : Patchy air space opacities are seen in both lung mid lower zones.
Diffuse ground glass opacities are also noted bilaterally.
Regression of disease process after 06 days.
Eventually patient became RT-PCR -ve and discharge home in stable condition.
Case Discussion : This patient tested positive for RT-PCR of SARS-COV2 thus was considered to have COVID-19 pneumonia.
