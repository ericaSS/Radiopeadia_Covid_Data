COVID-19 pneumonia AGE : 45 years, GENDER : Male
Presentation : Chest pain, cough, and dyspnea for two weeks.
CT : diffuse crazy paving in the right and left lungs.
multiple ground-glass opacities in the lower lobes, with peripheral distribution.
multiple prominent mediastinal lymph nodes
Case Discussion : Ground-glass opacities with interlobular septal thickening giving the crazy-paving appearance are the primary findings in COVID-19 pneumonia.
