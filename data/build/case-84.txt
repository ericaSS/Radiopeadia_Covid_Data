COVID-19 pneumonia - AGE : 40 years, GENDER : Male

X-ray : Bilateral air space patchy consolidations, mainly distributed in the right lung and left lower lung zone.
No pleural effusion.
No pneumothorax.
Cardiac size appears normal.
Case Discussion : This is a young male patient came with a typical history of COVID-19 :  fever and dry cough.
