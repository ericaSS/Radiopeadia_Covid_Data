COVID-19 pneumonia - AGE : 30 years, GENDER : Male
Presentation : Young diabetic patient presented with cough, shortness of breath and fever
X-ray : Bilateral patchy areas of air space consolidation with a lower zone predominance.
Case Discussion : This is a young high risk patient.
Radiological findings of COVID-19 affirmed with a positive RT-PCR analysis.
The manifestations are analyzed as 'typical' for COVID-19 with no further indication for CT.
The patient remained clinically stable and was released to home quarantine.
