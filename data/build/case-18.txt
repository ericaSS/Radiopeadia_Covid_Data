COVID-19 pneumonia - AGE : Adult, GENDER : Female
Presentation : Patient admitted for heart failure.
Developed fever and dyspnea during hospitalization.
X-ray : AP chest x-ray documents the presence of consolidation at both lung bases as well as in the peripheral mid zones.
The heart is enlarged.
Single-lead pacemaker and valvuloplasty noted.
Case Discussion : RT-PCR test was positive for COVID-19.
This case documents how, despite the "lockdown" established by many countries, hospital infections can affect patients especially if they are elderly.
