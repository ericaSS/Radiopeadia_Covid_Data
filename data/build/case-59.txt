COVID-19 pneumonia - AGE : 55 years, GENDER : Male
Presentation : Six days self-isolating with fever.
Progressive respiratory effort.
Lymphopenic on presentation (1.2).
X-ray : Mutifocal consolidation in right mid zone and left mid/lower zones.
No pleural abnormality.
12-hours after : Intensive care study; ET tube and right jugular central lines.
Progressive right upper and left lower mixed interstitial / airspace opacifications.
6-days : Persisting but improving regions of consolidation nearly a week after presentation.
Remains intubated.
Case Discussion : Imaging demonstrating the rapid deterioration on imaging in patients with COVID-19 with respiratory failure.
This patient was positive on PCR swab testing.
