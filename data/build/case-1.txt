COVID-19 pneumonia - 45 YEAR OLD
Presentation : Fever, cough and shortness of breath on arrival patient saturation of oxygen was 75%
X-ray : There is peripheral patchy air space opacification seen in both lung lower zones with diffuse ground-glass haze bilaterally.
Case Discussion
