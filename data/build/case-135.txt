COVID-19 pneumonia AGE : 1 years, GENDER : Female
Presentation : Fever, tachypnea, and non-productive cough that began 5 days before imaging.
CT : small areas of the right lung anteriorly, multi-focal peripheral patchy areas of ground-glass opacities are seen in the anterior segment of left upper lobe, lingual segments and an anteromedial segment of the left lower lobe.
Case Discussion : This patient had positive RT-PCR testing for COVID-19.
Ground glass pattern is the most common finding in COVID-19 infections, and they are usually multifocal bilateral and peripheral.
