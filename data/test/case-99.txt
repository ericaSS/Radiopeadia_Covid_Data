Outpatient incidental COVID-19 - AGE : 55 years, GENDER : Male
Presentation : Tired for the past 4 weeks with weight loss and reduced appetite.
Malignancy?
CT : Bilateral peripheral focal groundglass change in both lower lobes.
No lobar consolidation.
Small volume mediastinal lymphadenopathy.
No pleural effusion.
Case Discussion : Typical COVID-19 appearances at the lung bases on a CT CAP undertaken to look for an occult malignancy in a primary care patient.
This was one of several cases of incidental COVID-19 changes in outpatients and primary care patients experienced during the COVID-19 pandemic.
This highlights the large variability in symptoms experienced in those with this novel virus.
